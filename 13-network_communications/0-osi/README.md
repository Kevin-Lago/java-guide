# Network Communications

## OSI (Open Systems Interconnection)

- Also known as the Seven-layer Model

- A conceptual model that characterizes and standardizes the communication functions of a telecommunications or computing system without regard to its underlying internal structure and technology. Its goal is the interoperability of diverse communication systems with standard communication protocols
